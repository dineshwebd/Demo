ABOUT US

Aamir and Hameeda Interior Designers (AANDHI) deliver chic,refined onterior with luxurious finished, and, occasional, bursts of daring colour or modern geometric,AANDHis headed by the husband wife duo aamir and Hameed, their extensive and versatile body of work ranged from interior design of residences, corporate offices, retail stores and hospitality spaces They have in the past decadess of pracetice won up to 20National and Regional Award for Excellence in Design including the Hafele Trend Excellence Award in interior Design in 2016 and 2017 and have won many National Award by IIID
